Create an email asking a website owner to consider including a link in a blog post to a product. Write all output in [TARGETLANGUAGE]. Please find the product name in [PROMPT]. Open the email with "Hello!" Begin by complimenting the blog post in the first paragraph. In the second paragraph, explain why the product would be a good addition to the blog post. In a third paragraph, thank the reader for their consideration. End by signing off. Avoid references to building a partnership.
